About Me

From Curiosity to Creation: My AI Journey

I’m Samarth, and my story with technology started not in a classroom, but with a simple question: “How can I make things work better?” This question has guided me from tinkering with code in high school to building AI-powered tools that help real people and businesses today.

At Delhi Technological University, I found my passion for blending engineering with artificial intelligence. I’ve worked on projects that range from reducing waste in rural supply chains to building voice communication platforms and intuitive dashboards. Each project taught me that the best solutions come from listening first—understanding the real problem before writing a single line of code.

I believe in learning by doing, collaborating openly, and always asking “why not?” Whether I’m leading a team, diving into a new framework, or debugging at midnight, I’m driven by the excitement of turning ideas into impact.
